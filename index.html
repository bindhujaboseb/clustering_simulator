Educate Enlighten Empower
🔍
What is clustering?

Grouping similar data points together — like sorting fruit by colour without being told what the groups are.

🔢
What is K?

K = the number of groups you want. You choose K before the algorithm starts. Try K=2, 3, and 4!

🤖
How does it work?

Drop K centres → assign every dot to its nearest centre → move each centre to the middle → repeat!

🧠
Unsupervised?

Yes — no labels needed! The AI finds patterns on its own, just by measuring how close the points are.
